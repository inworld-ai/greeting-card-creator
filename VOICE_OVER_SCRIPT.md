## Key Talking Points (for customization)

- **Personalization**: Child's name and chosen topic make each story unique
- **AI-Powered**: Advanced AI creates engaging, age-appropriate stories
- **High-Quality Narration**: Professional AI voices bring stories to life
- **Shareable**: Easy link sharing for family and friends
- **Christmas Magic**: Classic holiday elements in every story
- **Custom Narrators**: Option to use your own voice
- **Robert Munsch Style**: Playful, energetic, and fun storytelling
- **Accessible**: Works on any device, anywhere

---

## Tone Guidelines

- **Warm and Magical**: Emphasize the wonder and joy
- **Clear and Engaging**: Easy to understand, not overly technical
- **Family-Friendly**: Appropriate for all ages
- **Enthusiastic but Not Overwhelming**: Let the product speak for itself
- **Personal**: Focus on the emotional connection and gift-giving aspect
